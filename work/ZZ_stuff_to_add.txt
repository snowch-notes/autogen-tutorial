### Streaming Considerations

When using `on_messages_stream()`, keep in mind:
- Handle partial responses gracefully in your UI
- Implement proper error handling for stream interruptions
- Consider buffering strategies for smooth display
- Plan for variable chunk sizes and timing
- Ensure your application can handle both complete and incomplete response states
